About us

We at Indorra, believe in authentic practices when it comes to giving our customers a rich Asian dining experience. We have satisfied thousands of customers coming from different backgrounds and cultures. Our intentionns are simple. We aim to cook the freshest of ingredients in the most authentic manner which result in our scrumptious delicacies. We hope that you would stop by with your friends and family to cherish this fine dining experience :)
